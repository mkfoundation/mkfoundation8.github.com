    1.Brief information

    The Executive Director, Higher Education Student's Financing board (HESFB) has announced
    the call for study loans to the 9th cohort of students loan for Academic year 2023/2024
    This year's applications will commence on monday 18th july 2023 to 18th september 2024


    2.Eligibility for loan sheme

    - One should be a Ugandan citizen
    - A student must have scored atleast two principle passes in his/her essential subjects
    - There should be a need for financial assistance


    3.Apply for Scholarship/ loan Scheme now

    - The first step is to email us and request for application form or request for a link to fill online, print it, fill it and then upload it to mkfoundation8@gmail.com and wait for guidlines and reference code.

    -Use the reference code to monitor your aplication status. Go back to website home page and put it where you see loan status and then check status

    - Your Scholarship will be processed in less than 144 hours, just wait for the Message.

    NOTE: You will be directed on how to do everything via the email above, uploading the filled form or you can fill the form online depending on your choice. You can even just upload your name, S.4&S.6 index, S.4 results, A level subject combination and results if out already, your ccontact, and the university and course of your choice.


    Good luck!!
